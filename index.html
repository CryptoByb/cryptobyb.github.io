What is blacklisting?

Tether and Circle can freeze addresses suspected of fraud, sanctions, or theft. Blacklisted addresses cannot send or receive tokens.

How it works

Calls blacklist functions directly on each stablecoin smart contract via public RPC nodes. No data is stored.

Covered chains

USDT: Ethereum, Tron (only chains where Tether enforces blacklisting on-chain). USDC: Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Polygon, Avalanche.
